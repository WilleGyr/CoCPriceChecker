What it does

Every item, fairly priced.

📊

Estimated SEK value per item

Ridge least-squares regression across every bundle in your library. The more data, the sharper the estimate.

🎯

Deal score per bundle

One number tells you if it's a steal or a trap. Above 1.0× and you're winning. Below it and Supercell is.

🕒

Bundle history

Track active offers, archive expired ones, mark what you bought. Spot patterns over months.

🔌

Zero dependencies

No npm, no build, no backend. ~1500 lines of vanilla JS. Open app.html and you're running.

💾

Local-first data

Everything saved in localStorage. Export to JSON anytime. Your data, your machine.

🌑

Dark mode by default

Designed for late-night Town Hall planning. Tabular numerics. Reads like a Bloomberg terminal for clashers.

The math

30 bundles. One linear system.

Each bundle is one equation. Trader prices link gems to items. Gem packs anchor gems to kronor. With enough bundles, the system is overdetermined — and we solve it the way every regression problem gets solved.

3 · price(Builder Potion) + 20 · price(Wall Ring) = 65 kr
